## Card 5: Exposure Lock

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════╗
║         EXPOSURE LOCK (AE/AF)        ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                      ║
║  1. TAP AND HOLD on subject          ║
║     until "AE/AF LOCK" appears       ║
║                                      ║
║  2. SLIDE UP/DOWN to adjust          ║
║     brightness if needed             ║
║                                      ║
║  3. Lock stays until you tap         ║
║     somewhere else                   ║
║                                      ║
║  WHERE TO LOCK:                      ║
║  • Interior: Wall surface (not       ║
║              bright windows)         ║
║  • Exterior: Building (not sky)      ║
║  • Details:  Directly on subject     ║
║                                      ║
║  RE-LOCK when moving between         ║
║  bright and dark areas!              ║
║                                      ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════╝
```

## Card 6: Movement Techniques

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════╗
║        SMOOTH MOVEMENT TIPS          ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                      ║
║  THE "GLIDE WALK":                   ║
║  • Bend knees slightly               ║
║  • Walk heel-to-toe (rolling)        ║
║  • Hold phone with BOTH hands        ║
║  • Elbows tight to body              ║
║  • Move at HALF normal speed         ║
║  • Clear path first!                 ║
║                                      ║
║  PANS (Side to side):                ║
║  • Rotate from waist, not arms       ║
║  • Left to right preferred           ║
║  • 5-7 seconds for 90 degrees        ║
║                                      ║
║  RULE: If it feels too slow,         ║
║        it's probably right!          ║
║                                      ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════╝
```
