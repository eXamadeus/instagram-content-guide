# Content Strategy & Ideas

## Content Pillar Framework

### What Are Content Pillars?

Content pillars are 3-5 core themes that ensure consistent, relevant content aligned to business goals.

### Recommended Pillars

| Pillar | % of Content | Purpose | Content Types |
|--------|--------------|---------|---------------|
| **Transformation & Results** | 35% | Lead generation | Before/after, reveals, time-lapses |
| **Expertise & Education** | 25% | Authority building | How-to, craftsmanship, tips |
| **Culture & Team** | 20% | Recruitment, brand | Team spotlights, BTS |
| **Client Stories** | 15% | Conversion | Testimonials, walkthroughs |
| **Entertainment** | 5% | Reach | Trending content, humor |

## High-Performing Content Types

### 1. Transformation Reveals

**Why it works:** Creates anticipation, demonstrates results, shareable

**Formats:**
- Side-by-side before/after
- "Day 1 vs Day 90" progression
- Dramatic reveal with trending audio

### 2. Time-Lapses

**Why it works:** Compresses months into seconds, satisfying, high completion rates

**Best subjects:**
- Demolition
- Framing
- Flooring installation
- Painting

### 3. Before/After Comparisons

**Why it works:** Most shareable content - viewers send to friends

**Formats:**
- Carousel (swipe through)
- Reel with transition effect
- Side-by-side static image

### 4. Educational Content

**Why it works:** Save-worthy, positions as expert, consideration-stage nurturing

**Topics:**
- "Why we do it this way"
- Material comparisons
- Common mistakes to avoid
- What to expect in renovations

### 5. Problem/Solution Stories

**Why it works:** Shows expertise, builds trust, relatable

**Format:**
- "The hidden issue we found..."
- "Here's how we solved it..."

### 6. Behind-the-Scenes

**Why it works:** Builds connection, authenticity, recruitment

**Content:**
- Day-in-the-life
- Team moments
- Morning routines
- Job site culture

## Key Takeaways

1. **Transformation content is king** - Prioritize before/after reveals
2. **Shares are the top ranking signal** - Create content people send to friends
3. **Educational content gets saved** - Positions you as expert
4. **Team content supports recruitment** - Humanizes the brand
5. **Consistency beats perfection** - 3-4 good posts/week beats sporadic great posts
6. **Best times: 3-6 PM weekdays** - When homeowners browse
