# Instagram Best Practices (2024-2025)

## Algorithm Overview

Instagram uses **multiple algorithms** - different systems for Feed, Reels, Stories, and Explore.

### Key Ranking Signals (In Order)

1. **Watch Time** - How long people view your content
2. **Shares/Sends** - Top signal for reach (Instagram prioritizes content that "brings people together")
3. **Saves** - Indicates high-value content
4. **Comments** - Meaningful interactions
5. **Likes** - Still matters but less than shares/saves

### What This Means for Construction Content

**Focus on creating content people want to:**
- **Share** with friends planning renovations (transformation reveals)
- **Save** for future reference (educational tips, before/after inspiration)
- **Watch to completion** (satisfying reveals, time-lapses)

## Reels Best Practices

### Optimal Length

| Duration | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| 15-30 seconds | Quick reveals, satisfying clips |
| 30-60 seconds | Transformations, tutorials |
| 60-90 seconds | Complex stories, walkthroughs |

**Rule:** Under 90 seconds performs best.

### The First 3 Seconds (Critical)

The hook determines if viewers watch or scroll. Start with:

- Something visually striking
- A question or curiosity gap
- Text overlay that promises value
- The "before" of a dramatic transformation

**Examples:**
- "Wait until you see the after..."
- "This $200K kitchen was built in 6 weeks"
- [Shot of destroyed space] â†’ "3 months later..."

### Performance Boosters

| Do This | Avoid This |
|---------|------------|
| Add captions/subtitles | Low-resolution video |
| Include audio (music or voice) | TikTok watermarks |
| Use original, non-watermarked content | No audio at all |
| Use creative tools (text, effects) | Too much text |
| Loop your Reels (increases watch time) | Recycled content from other apps |

### Reels vs. Other Formats

| Format | Reach Potential | Best Use |
|--------|-----------------|----------|
| **Reels** | Highest | Discovery, transformations, viral potential |
| **Carousels** | Medium-High | Educational, detailed info, save-worthy |
| **Single images** | Medium | Portfolio shots, team photos |
| **Stories** | Followers only | Daily updates, BTS, polls |

## Posting Strategy

### Best Times (Construction Industry)

Based on analysis of 1M+ posts:

| Day | Best Times |
|-----|------------|
| Monday | 3-9 PM |
| Tuesday | 5-8 AM, **3-7 PM** (peak) |
| Wednesday | **5 PM** |
| Thursday | **4-5 PM** |
| Friday | 4 PM |
| Saturday | 11 AM, 5 PM |
| Sunday | 12-3 PM |

**For B2C home services:** Peak engagement is late afternoon (3-6 PM) when homeowners finish work.

### Recommended Weekly Schedule (3-4 Posts)

| Day | Time | Content Type |
|-----|------|--------------|
| Tuesday | 4-5 PM | Reel (transformation/before-after) |
| Thursday | 4-5 PM | Reel (process/BTS) or Carousel (tips) |
| Saturday | 11 AM | Reel (finished project showcase) |
| *Optional* Wednesday | 5 PM | Carousel (educational content) |

### Format Mix

- **60% Reels** - Transformations, process videos, reveals
- **30% Carousels** - Project walkthroughs, design tips, before/after galleries
- **10% Single images** - Hero shots, team content
- **Stories daily** - Job site updates, polls, Q&A, Reel reposts

## Hashtag Strategy

### Do Hashtags Still Matter?

**Yes, but less than before.** Keywords/SEO in captions are now more important.

### Optimal Number

**3-5 relevant hashtags** per post (Instagram's official recommendation)

### Strategy: Mix Broad + Niche + Location

**Broad (high volume):**
- #homerenovation
- #homedesign
- #renovation
- #interiordesign
- #beforeandafter

**Niche (targeted):**
- #kitchenremodel
- #bathroomrenovation
- #luxuryrenovation
- #residentialrenovation
- #homecontractor

**Location (essential for local business):**
- #[city]renovation
- #[city]contractor
- #[city]homedesign
- #[city]remodel

### Placement

Caption or first comment - no significant difference. Keep captions clean by placing hashtags in first comment if using many.

## Caption Optimization

### First Line = Most Important

This appears in preview before "more" button. Make it count:

- **Hook with curiosity:** "The one thing most homeowners forget..."
- **Hook with result:** "This kitchen took 6 weeks and $85K"
- **Hook with question:** "Would you have chosen marble or quartz?"

### Caption Structure

```
[Hook - visible in preview]

[Story or value - 2-3 sentences]

[Call-to-action]

[Hashtags - 3-5]
```

### Keywords for SEO

Instagram search now indexes caption text. Include naturally:
- "[City] home renovation"
- "Kitchen remodel"
- "Bathroom renovation"
- "Luxury renovation"
- "Residential contractor"

## Instagram SEO

### Profile Optimization

**Name field:** Include keywords
- Example: "Smith Renovations | Luxury Home Remodeling"

**Bio:** Include location + services
- Example: "High-end residential renovations in [City]. Kitchens | Bathrooms | Whole Home. Free consultations."

**Link in bio:** Use trackable link (UTM parameters)

### Alt Text

Add descriptive alt text to every image:
1. Tap "Advanced settings" when posting
2. Add alt text describing the image
3. Include keywords naturally (don't stuff)

**Example:** "Modern white kitchen with marble countertops and gold hardware in Atlanta home renovation"

## Call-to-Action Strategy

### CTA Types by Goal

| Goal | CTA Examples |
|------|--------------|
| Lead generation | "Book your free consultation - link in bio" |
| Engagement | "Save this for your renovation inspiration" |
| Reach | "Share with someone planning a kitchen reno" |
| Recruitment | "DM 'CAREER' for current openings" |
| Website traffic | "See the full tour - link in bio" |

### CTA Placement

- **In video:** Lower-third text in last 5 seconds
- **Caption:** End of caption, after value delivery
- **Bio:** Clear action ("Book free consultation")

### Effective CTA Formulas

**For lead generation:**
"Planning a renovation? DM us 'START' for our free planning checklist"

**For engagement:**
"Save this for your renovation mood board"

**For shares:**
"Tag a friend who needs to see this transformation"

## Engagement Best Practices

### Responding to Comments

- **Reply within first hour** if possible
- Every reply counts as additional engagement
- Ask follow-up questions to continue conversation
- Use replies to add value (answer questions fully)

### Engaging with Others

- Interact with complementary businesses (architects, designers, real estate)
- Comment meaningfully on relevant posts
- This helps algorithm understand your niche

### Collaboration Opportunities

- **Instagram Collabs** - Co-shared authorship splits engagement
- Partner with: Interior designers, real estate agents, home stagers
- Both accounts get reach benefit

### What to Avoid

- Engagement bait ("Like for..." or "Comment 'YES'")
- Follow/unfollow schemes
- Purchased followers or engagement
- Excessive tagging or irrelevant hashtags

## Metrics to Track

### Weekly Review (15 min)

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| Reach | How many saw content |
| Views/Plays | Reel performance |
| Engagement rate | Quality of content |
| Saves | High-value content indicator |
| Shares | Viral potential |
| Profile visits | CTA effectiveness |

### Benchmarks

| Metric | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|---------|------|-----------|
| Engagement rate | 1-3% | 3-5% | 5%+ |
| Reel views vs followers | 1:1 | 2:1 | 5:1+ |
| Save rate | 1-2% | 2-4% | 4%+ |

### Monthly Review (1 hour)

1. Compare content types (which pillar performed best?)
2. Review best posting times for YOUR audience
3. Analyze top 3 and bottom 3 posts
4. Track follower growth
5. Count DM inquiries and conversions
