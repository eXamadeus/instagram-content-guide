## Worker/Employee Media Release

```
EMPLOYEE/CONTRACTOR MEDIA RELEASE

I, _________________________________ ("Participant"), 
voluntarily grant [Company Name] ("Company") permission to:

1. Photograph, video record, and audio record my image, 
   likeness, and voice during company work activities.

2. Use such recordings for:
   - Social media marketing (Instagram, Facebook, etc.)
   - Company website and promotional materials
   - Training and educational purposes
   - Industry publications and submissions

3. Edit, modify, and use the recordings without 
   additional compensation beyond regular wages/fees.

I UNDERSTAND THAT:

- My participation is VOLUNTARY
- I may decline to be photographed/recorded on specific 
  occasions without penalty
- I will not receive additional compensation for media use
- I release the Company from claims arising from 
  authorized use
- This release does not affect my employment status


RESTRICTIONS (if any): _________________________________

___________________________________________________

Participant Signature          Date

_____________________________
Printed Name

Relationship to Company:
[ ] Employee  [ ] Subcontractor  [ ] Other: ____________

Company Representative         Date

Printed Name & Title
```
